Puppies

Puppies are young dogs known for their playful energy, curiosity, and affectionate nature. They require proper care, including feeding, socialization, and training, to grow into well-behaved adult dogs. During their early months, puppies learn important behaviors and form strong bonds with their human companions. Their cuteness and lively personalities make them popular pets around the world.

Kitties

Kitties, or kittens, are young cats that are playful, curious, and full of energy. They rely on their mother or human caregivers for nourishment, warmth, and safety in their early weeks. As they grow, they develop hunting instincts, learn to groom themselves, and become more independent. Their soft fur, tiny paws, and playful antics make them beloved companions for many people.

Bunnies

Bunnies are small, gentle animals known for their long ears, soft fur, and powerful hind legs. They are social creatures that enjoy companionship and need space to hop and explore. Bunnies have a diet that mainly consists of hay, fresh vegetables, and clean water to stay healthy. Their calm nature and cute appearance make them popular pets, especially among families.
